Feloria Main Story, Quest, Dialogue and Icon Integration

You are expanding the Feloria project into a full narrative monster-collection RPG with exploration, dialogue, capture, battle, codex, evolution, quests, and legendary events.

The story should be inspired by the structure of classic handheld monster RPGs but must remain completely original in characters, world, names, and dialogue.

The project already includes exploration and battle systems. Your task is to implement a structured narrative system including quest progression, rival encounters, NPC dialogue, legendary story events, and shop items with icons.

World Name: Feloria

Feloria is a world inhabited by intelligent feline creatures.
